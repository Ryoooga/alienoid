ABOUT US

PT Bricks Group Indonesia is group of young traders who have experienced the various commodities, engaged in domestic and international trade.
Through the creation of business ecosystem with strategic partnership which creates harmonization of supply-demand from upstream to downstream (end to end), added value processes, and measurable commercialization for the trading of various products and commodities, export and import.

We believe in connecting people worldwide. We strive to work with various companies by accepting their good culture and help them to work flexibly as per their convenience.
We also support government programs in cooperative partnerships with UMKM (Usaha Mikro, Kecil, dan Menengah) – Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises through the principle of mutual benefit.
The goal is for UMKM to enter the global production chain, and increase the opportunities for UMKM to upgrade.
